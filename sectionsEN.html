The Kingdom and its tourism role

The Kingdom is investing heavily in the tourism sector, to support an increase in the gross domestic product in the coming years, and among the most prominent investments are the tourism and entertainment projects represented by the Amala project, the Red Sea project, the future city project “NEOM”, and the Qiddiya tourist city project, in addition to entertainment seasons such as Riyadh Season, Jeddah Season, and many more events. Saudi Arabia is also working on establishing entertainment cities and developing islands and tourist destinations to suit foreign tourists, as it is undertaking many initiatives to develop ancient tourist islands such as the Farasan Islands and develop coastal beaches.

Tourist areas

There are many tourist areas in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, each of which has distinct aspects. There are 13 main administrative regions in the Kingdom, which are:
1. Riyadh Region: It is considered a major economic and cultural center in the Kingdom, and is famous for its rich history and modern buildings.
2. Makkah Al-Mukarramah Region: It contains the Holy Mosque in Mecca and the Prophet’s Mosque and is considered a major religious center of Islam.
3. Medina District: Home to the Prophet’s Mosque and many historical and religious sites.
4. Al-Sharqiyah Region: It is famous for its oil industry, popular culture, and seafood.
5. Asir region: It contains the mountains of the Asir Plateau, stunning landscapes, and archaeological sites.
6. Tabuk region: It is characterized by its beautiful beaches and wild landscapes.
7. Hail Region: Famous for Bedouin culture and traditional festivals.
8. Jazan region: It contains beautiful beaches, farms, and mountains.
9. Najran region: It is famous for its rich history and archaeological ruins.
10. Al-Baha Region: It includes stunning mountains and charming landscapes.
11. Al-Jawf region: It is characterized by deserts and historical sites.
12. The Northern Border Region: It includes archaeological sites and a rich popular culture.
13. Asir region: It contains the mountains of the Asir Plateau, stunning landscapes, and archaeological sites.

Monuments and landmarks

*There are many famous monuments and landmarks in Saudi Arabia:
1. Deira Archaeological Site: The Deira area in Riyadh is considered one of the most important archaeological sites in the Kingdom, and includes many historical monuments and buildings.
2. Medina City: It includes many important religious sites such as the Prophet’s Mosque and the Dome of the Rock.
3. The Holy City of Mecca: It includes the Holy Mosque of Mecca and the Holy Kaaba, and is considered the home of Hajj and Umrah.
4. Jeddah City: It includes the historical country and many historical buildings and monuments.
5. Diriyah City: It includes many historical buildings and traditional markets.
6. Al-Ahsa City: It includes palm plantations and historical castles.
7. Mada’in Saleh Site: It is considered one of the most important archaeological sites in the Kingdom, and it is registered on the UNESCO World Heritage List.
8. Al-Masmak Palace: It is considered one of the most important historical monuments in the Asir region.
